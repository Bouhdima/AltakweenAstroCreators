Who We Are

We are a team of educators, developers, and students, united by a passion for blending science, art, and technology. Our project, “Flara’s Big Journey,” is a bilingual digital story designed to teach children about space weather through immersive, interactive storytelling. Flara, a friendly solar flare, guides young readers as they explore how solar storms impact life on Earth. Featuring animations, sound effects, and direct links to real NASA data, the story makes complex science engaging and easy to understand. Our goal is to spark curiosity, foster learning, and make the wonders of space weather accessible and fun for all.
